About Nampak Malawi

50 years powering Malawi's packaging industry

Our History

Nampak Malawi was established in 1974 as a subsidiary of Nampak Ltd, Africa's largest diversified packaging manufacturer. From our Chichiri, Blantyre facility, we have grown from a single corrugated carton line to a full-spectrum packaging plant serving Malawi, Zambia, and the broader COMESA region.

In 2024, following a strategic portfolio review by the parent group, Nampak Malawi transitioned to independent ownership via a private equity consortium, reaffirming the long-term commitment to the Malawian market.

Corporate Social Responsibility

We directly employ 320 Malawian workers and support a further 1,200 indirect jobs in the local supply chain. Our apprenticeship programme has trained over 180 young Malawians since 2010.

Our Blantyre facility runs a zero-landfill initiative, converting 98% of production waste into recycled feedstock or biomass fuel.
